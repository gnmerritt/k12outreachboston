Volunteer in programs serving K-12 students in Boston

Welcome! Our mission is to make information about K12 outreach programs in Boston and the Massachusetts area more accessible to volunteers and parents, alike, by centralizing the information about these programs into one place. We are a group of scientists and engineers from the Boston and Cambridge areas who are working to help outreach programs gain volunteers and support, and to help parents find affordable activities and educational opportunities for their children. Please feel free to email {{ 'k12outreachboston@gmail.com' | obfuscate_mailto }} if you have any questions, comments, suggestions, or concerns, and importantly, if you notice that we have not included a program or event that you think should be added to the site!
